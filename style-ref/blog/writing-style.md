# Blog Writing Style

English essay for korean-teacher-mj `/blog`. Korean snippets optional via [[KO: ...]].

## Goal
Opinionated essay with a new thesis. Not a summary.

## Do
- Mixed paragraph lengths, contractions
- **Bold full key sentences** — 1–2 per section (added in de-AI pass 4)
- Plain ## section titles (no colons)

## Don't
- Semicolons (;)
- AI phrasebook: cognitive debt, literacy of attention, taste intelligence (see de-claude.md)
- Uniform polish — one slightly rough sentence per section is good
- Listicles, fake stories, report tone
