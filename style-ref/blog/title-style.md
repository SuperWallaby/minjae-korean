# Blog Title Style

Hook with contradiction or reframing — not a list count.

Good:
- "I Thought Studying More Vocab Was the Answer. It Wasn't."
- "My Take on Why Korean Feels Hard After Years of English Class"

Bad:
- "5 Tips from This YouTube Video"
- "Everything You Need to Know About..."

Use **I / my** when it signals a take, not a ranking.
